Dark total card stays solid (it IS the content here). The primary confirm button floats inside a glass pill at the bottom — still obviously terracotta, but now it lifts off a translucent surface rather than a flat bar.

Liquid Glass rules followed

  • Glass only on navigation & chrome — nav bars, segmented controls, search, cart bar, action bar, FAB.
  • Content (hero, cards, list rows, total card, method tiles) stays solid so prices and modifiers don't fight refracted color.
  • Specular top highlight on every glass surface: inset 0 1px 0 rgba(255,255,255,0.85).
  • Concentric corners — outer pills at 999px with 999px inner segment chips; capsule bars at 28px with pill buttons inside.

Materials

  • Regular glass: rgba(255,250,243,0.52) + blur(30px) saturate(180%).
  • Dark glass (optional for dark mode): rgba(28,20,16,0.55) + same blur.
  • Tinted glass (primary CTA FAB): terracotta with bright specular highlight and 22px pill shadow.
  • Canvas has warm saffron + terracotta radial blobs so the glass actually looks like glass (it needs color behind it to refract).

Accessibility guard-rails

  • 44pt minimum tap targets on every glass control (nav buttons 40pt + hitSlop, pills 40pt tall, FAB 56pt).
  • Text on glass is ink-900 — 12:1 contrast on the lightened backdrop.
  • If the user enables Reduce Transparency, the glass falls back to solid bone — already planned for the RN port.
  • NN/g's readability concerns addressed by keeping content on solid surfaces.
